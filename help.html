Help & Tutorials

Learn how to use the ATS Resume Matcher and understand why keyword optimization is important.

1. How to Use the ATS Resume Matcher

Navigate to the Tool page and paste your resume text into the first box and the job description into the second box. Click Compare to see your match score and a list of keywords that are present in the job description but missing from your resume. Use this information to update your resume before submitting your application.

2. Why Missing Keywords Matter

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) scan resumes for specific terms related to the job. If your resume lacks important keywords or skills, it may be filtered out before a human ever sees it. Including relevant keywords ensures your resume passes the initial screening.

3. Why ATS Optimization is Important

With more than 97% of large companies using ATS software and 75% of resumes never reaching recruiters, optimizing your resume has become essential. Proper optimization gives you a better chance of being selected for an interview by ensuring your resume ranks well against the job description.

4. Resume Structure Do’s & Don’ts

Keep your resume format simple: use standard section headings, avoid graphics or tables that might confuse ATS parsing algorithms, and maintain consistent date formats. A clear, linear structure makes it easier for ATS to extract relevant information.

5. Common Resume Mistakes

Avoid typos, overly long resumes, and unquantified achievements. Make sure your resume is tailored for each job by emphasizing relevant skills and experiences.
